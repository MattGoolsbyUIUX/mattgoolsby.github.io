About me

I understand people before I design for them.

I spent 4 years in 360 recruiting — learning to listen closely, translate between two sides who don't always speak the same language, and make complex human decisions feel effortless. Now I bring that same people-first thinking to product design.

I'm also a Qigong teacher, a former acupuncturist, and a Japanese speaker based in Tokyo. My background isn't typical for a designer — but understanding people at that depth is exactly what makes good design possible.
